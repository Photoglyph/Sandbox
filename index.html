Welcome to Our Page

We are committed to ornithology; both as a pursuit and a means of studying the winged creatures who inhabit our world. Our site contains a great deal of information about many species; their habitats, migratory patterns and distribution.

We also have data/airspeed velocity on the unladen swallow; both African and European!

So, don't waste time! Get out and get what you need! Turn off that computer and get out into the great outdoors!
